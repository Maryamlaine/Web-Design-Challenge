Max Temperature

As expected the weather becomes significantly warmer as one approaches the equator(0 Deg. Latitude). More interstingly, however is the fact that the southeren hemisphere tends to be warmer in this time of the year than northern hemisphere.This may be due to the tilt of the earth at the time of the year this data was gathered.
